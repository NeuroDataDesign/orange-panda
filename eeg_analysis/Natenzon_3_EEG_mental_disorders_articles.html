Diagnosis of Psychiatric Disorders Using EEG Data and Employing a Statistical Decision Model

Quick Summary

Background

  • Hard to differentiate between:
    • Psychotic depression vs. Schizophrenia
    • Major depressive disorder vs. Bipolar depression
  • Uses feature selection and mixture of factor analysis
    • Attains over 85% accuracy

Relevance to Us

  • Somewhat accurately differentiates between healthy participants, and those with mental disorders
  • Includes mathematical representations of statistical operations used
  • Similar sized data set

Data Format

  • 207 subjects
    • 64 major depressive disorder
    • 40 with schizophrenia
    • 12 bipolar depression
    • 91 healthy subjects
  • Analysis
    • Feature selection method of Peng
    • Mixture of factor analysis
    • Output is likelihood of subject having a particular mental disorder
